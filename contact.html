A few useful answers.

Do you work internationally?

Yes. Sedisson works with companies across different markets, including teams planning regional or international growth.

How long do engagements last?

Growth Foundation usually runs for 3 months, Growth Authority for 3–6 months and Growth Acceleration for 6–12 months.

Do you accept every business?

No. If the business would benefit more from preparatory work than an acceleration programme, we will say so.

Why does the assessment ask for this much detail?

Because it lets us prepare properly. You should not have to spend the first meeting reciting information we could have read beforehand.
