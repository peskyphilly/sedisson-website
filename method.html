The Sedisson Method™

Fix the right problem.
In the right order.

Our method gives a complicated commercial problem a sensible sequence: understand what is happening, decide what must change, then build from there.

How the work unfolds

The order matters.

There is little value in scaling a message buyers do not understand. And there is no point driving attention to a business that has not yet earned their confidence. We deal with the foundations first.

01 / 06

Assess

First, we work out what is actually happening.

We look closely at the business, the market, the customer and the way new revenue is currently won. The aim is to separate the visible symptoms from the problem underneath.

  • Commercial audit
  • Market research
  • Product evaluation
  • Competitor analysis
  • Readiness assessment
  • Client qualification

How we make decisions

01

Look before prescribing.

Recommendations should come from customers, competitors and performance—not a favourite playbook.

02

Give buyers a reason to believe.

Before creating more attention, make sure the proof can stand up to it.

03

Learn while doing.

Every campaign should leave the team knowing something it can use next time.
